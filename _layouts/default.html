Sanjoy Chowdhury (page in progress)

I am working as a Machine Learning Scientist with the Camera and Video AI team at ShareChat, India

From June 2019 to June 2021, I worked at Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore, as part of Vision Intelligence Group. I primarily worked on developing novel AI powered solutions for different products of Samsung

I have an MTech in Computer Science & Engineering (AI focus) from IIIT Hyderabad, where I was a research assistant for Prof. Ravi Kiran Sarvadevabhatla and a teaching assistant for Prof. Ashok Kumar Das for the courses Statistical Methods in AI and Discrete Mathematics and Algorithms respectively. During my days at IIIT-H, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. C V Jawahar.
